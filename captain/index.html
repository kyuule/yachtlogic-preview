Professional Captain Service

The Yacht Logic team provides professional captain service for the short or long term. All captains are licensed Masters with extensive experience in diverse cruising grounds.

Experience

Our captains have experience in the best charter boat cruising grounds:

  • Caribbean — Virgin Islands, Leeward and Windward Islands, Bahamas
  • Pacific Northwest — Puget Sound, San Juan Islands, Gulf Islands
  • Alaska — Inside Passage, Southeast Alaska, Prince William Sound
  • Mexico — Sea of Cortez, Pacific Coast
  • Central America — Costa Rica, Panama
  • East Coast — Maine to Florida, Chesapeake Bay, Intracoastal Waterway

Ocean Passages

Our captains are experienced in major ocean passages including:

  • Trans-Pacific to and from Hawaii
  • Trans-Atlantic crossings
  • Panama Canal transits
  • High latitude expeditions (Canada, Alaska, Antarctica, Patagonia)

Services Available

Our captains can serve as:

  • Cruise Guide — Navigate unfamiliar waters with local knowledge
  • Navigator — Professional passage planning and watchkeeping
  • Instructor — Teach you to operate your own vessel
  • Ice Pilot — High latitude experience in Arctic and Antarctic waters
  • Relief Captain — Fill in for your yacht’s permanent captain

Rates

Daily Rate: $300 plus travel expenses

All captains hold USCG licenses and appropriate endorsements for the vessels they operate.
