About Yacht Logic

About Adam Jenkins

Owner — Adam Jenkins, age 41, began sailing as a young boy and acquired his Master Mariners license at 18 years old. He started working as a captain aboard Puget Sound and San Juan Island charter yachts.

The Journey

In 1992, Adam embarked on a single-handed voyage down the Pacific Coast aboard Saint Brendan, his 27 foot sloop. After transiting the Panama Canal, he circumnavigated the Caribbean and explored the eastern seaboard.

During this four year cruise, Adam made a living as a professional charter and delivery captain, gaining invaluable experience in diverse waters and conditions.

Founding Yacht Logic

Returning to Seattle in 1996, Adam started Yacht Logic, primarily focusing on yacht delivery and professional captain service.

In the past decade, Yacht Logic has become one of the top yacht delivery companies in the USA. Vessels delivered or managed range from 27 to 300 feet.

Science & Research

Adam has also provided science support for ocean-going research projects for:

  • NOAA — National Oceanic and Atmospheric Administration
  • USAP — United States Antarctic Program
  • NMFS — National Marine Fisheries Service
  • MBARI — Monterey Bay Aquarium Research Institute

Experience spans from Arctic to Antarctica.

Home Base

Adam and his daughter Sophia live in San Diego, California.

Licenses & Certifications

  • STCW95 endorsement
  • Auxiliary Sailing & Towing endorsements
  • NOAA certified working diver
  • NAUI certified Master Diver
