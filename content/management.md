## Professional Vessel Management

Vessel management requires expertise, flexibility, and technical knowledge. Yacht Logic believes the goal of the yacht manager is to **maintain a ship in ready-to-depart condition at practical costs.**

### Our Management Services

**Routine Operations:**
- Routine maintenance schedule for all vessel systems
- Regular inspection of ongoing maintenance schedules
- Support for spares/chandlery/purveying/fuel bunkering while underway

**Refits & Repairs:**
- Coordination of refits, repairs, and maintenance
- Evaluation of suppliers and contractors
- Scheduled maintenance and repair scenarios
- Dry dockings and major refits

**Administrative Support:**
- Insurance claims management
- Documentation and compliance
- Budget planning and cost management

### Our Approach

We work with owners to develop maintenance programs that:

- Keep vessels safe and seaworthy
- Maintain systems in reliable operating condition
- Control costs through preventive maintenance
- Minimize downtime and maximize cruising time

### Vessels Managed

We have managed vessels ranging from 27 to 300 feet, including:

- Private yachts (sail and power)
- Charter vessels
- Research vessels
- Commercial craft

Our experience includes science support for ocean-going research projects for NOAA, USAP, NMFS, and MBARI — from Arctic to Antarctica.
