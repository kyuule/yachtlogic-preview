**The Expedition Yacht Peregrine** — explore the beauty and maritime history of the Pacific Northwest.

### Expedition Destinations

Personalized expeditions through:

- **San Juan Islands** — Washington's jewel of the Pacific Northwest
- **Majestic Inside Passage of BC** — Thousands of miles of protected waterways
- **Queen Charlotte Islands (Haida Gwaii)** — Remote wilderness and indigenous culture
- **Southeast Alaska** — Glaciers, wildlife, and frontier communities

### About the Peregrine

The Peregrine was designed and built specifically to operate in remote areas — conceived, built and launched in 2003 by Bigfoot Marine.

**Design Philosophy:**
- Designed to be the quietest possible boat
- Utilizes sail-assist innovations for an eco-friendly yacht
- With Genoa and tri-sail, she is a sailor's power yacht
- Purpose-built for expedition cruising in challenging environments

### Charter Options
