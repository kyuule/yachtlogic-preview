## Professional Maritime Services Since 1996

Yacht Logic provides comprehensive maritime services including yacht delivery, professional captain services, expedition charters, and vessel management. Based in San Diego, California, we have delivered and managed vessels ranging from 27 to 300 feet across the globe.
